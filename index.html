Welcome to the ALTEQ Website

ALTEQ is a cryptographic signature algorithm based on the hardness of the alternating trilinear form equivalence problem submitted to the NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography Standardization Process.

News

15/09/2023 - Version 1.1 of spec and implementation released.

14/09/2023 - Version 1.0.1 of spec and implementation released.

17/07/2023 - ALTEQ is accepted by NIST as a complete and
                     proper submission.

01/06/2023 - ALTEQ is submitted to the NIST standardization process.
